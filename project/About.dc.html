Why we exist

Most founder-led service businesses are running on duct tape.

Spreadsheets nobody trusts. Tools that don't talk to each other. Quotes at 11pm. The founder still chasing every important thing.

We've seen this pattern in heating engineers turning over £2M, in fire and security firms managing fifty engineers, in training providers drowning in admin. Same problem, different overalls.

We built Calon to fix it properly. Not with a software platform. Not with consultancy theatre. With practical operating systems built around how the business already works.

How we work

Six principles. We hold to them.

We build around your real workflow.

No rip-and-replace. No "throw out ServiceM8 and use ours." We connect to what already works and fix what doesn't.

Phase 1 in six to eight weeks.

Not six months. You see real systems running inside two months of starting, not a Powerpoint of what could be.

One UK lead per engagement.

You always have one person accountable. Not an account manager. The actual lead.

We stay after the build.

Ongoing retainer to keep systems working as the business grows. Not "delivered, here's the invoice, good luck."

AI agents only where they create value.

Not because it's fashionable. If the right answer is a workflow with no AI, that's what we ship.

We turn down work we can't do well.

If you're not founder-led, or there's no operational friction to fix, or the business isn't ready, we say so. Saves everyone time.
