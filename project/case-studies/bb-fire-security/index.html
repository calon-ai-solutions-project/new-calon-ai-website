Fire and Security

BB Fire and Security

Fire and security installation and maintenance. NSI Gold, BAFE, ISO 9001. Running on Uptick.

The situation

BB Fire and Security already had an operational backbone in place through Uptick, their job management platform. The position from the senior team was clear from the first conversation. This was not a rip-and-replace project. The goal was to add a practical AI layer around the existing system that would speed up sales response, sharpen quote follow-up, capture every incoming call, and take admin weight off the senior team.

The cost of the gap

Inbound enquiries were not getting same-day responses consistently. Quotes were being sent and then forgotten, with no automated follow-up. Phone calls outside office hours were going to voicemail or being missed entirely, with no visibility on what was being lost. The senior team was carrying too much of the daily admin load personally.

What we built

A three-layer AI build sitting cleanly around Uptick, not in place of it.

  • AI Quote Assistant. Drafts professional responses to inbound enquiries in BB's tone of voice, ready for senior review and one-click send.
  • Operations Layer. Gives the team live visibility on every enquiry and every quote, with structured follow-up workflows running in the background.
  • Lisa, a custom AI voice receptionist. Answers calls 24/7 using cloned voice via ElevenLabs, three intelligent call flows on Vapi infrastructure, and conditional forwarding to the senior team only when it matters.

Results

  • Every inbound call now answered, day or night
  • Quote response time cut from hours to minutes
  • Senior team admin load reduced significantly
  • Lisa live within three weeks of project start. Full operations layer live in four to six weeks
  • Total investment: £13,997 setup, £647 per month ongoing

The principle that landed with the BB team

AI works best when it is added around the systems a business already trusts. The goal is never to replace good infrastructure, it is to make that infrastructure faster, sharper, and harder to lose money from.
