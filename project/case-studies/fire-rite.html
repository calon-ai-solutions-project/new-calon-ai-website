Fire Rite

Fire and security. Cardiff. Simpro platform. BS 5839 and BAFE compliance.

The situation

Fire Rite is a Cardiff-based fire and security firm running on Simpro, with a qualified estimating team handling compliance-driven quotation work. They had built a strong library of compliant historical quotes over the years, but the cognitive load of producing each new quote in line with the same compliance pattern was slowing the sales cycle and tying up senior estimating capacity.

The cost of that bottleneck

Every new compliance-led quote was effectively being written from scratch, even when very similar work had been quoted before. The documentation requirements under BS 5839 and BAFE meant senior approval was non-negotiable, but the drafting time leading up to that approval was eating estimator hours the business needed back. There was also a clear constraint. Nothing in the stack could be replaced. Fire Rite was committed to Simpro and already working with Configur on the data and analytics layer. The brief was to add value alongside both, not in competition with either.

What we built

A workflow automation layer that extracts pricing and equipment data from the existing library of compliant historical quotation PDFs and structures new quotes following the same compliance pattern.

  • AI handles the cognitive lift of pattern matching, equipment selection and pricing assembly
  • Pricing follows the same logic Fire Rite's senior estimators already trust, so output stays inside their compliance discipline
  • The qualified estimating team retains final approval on every quote, so compliance authority stays exactly where it should
  • Calon sits alongside Simpro and Configur in the stack. Simpro continues to run jobs. Configur continues to surface insights across the data. Calon handles the workflow gap in between.

Results

  • Estimating team capacity freed up for higher-value work
  • Quote turnaround sharpened without compromising on BS 5839 or BAFE compliance
  • Clear proof point that AI can sit cleanly inside a regulated workflow without replacing the human judgment that compliance demands
