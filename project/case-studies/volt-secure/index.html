The situation

Cai Llewellyn was running Volt Secure's quoting out of his personal ChatGPT account. Job tracking, customer records, engineer schedules and quote follow-up were spread across spreadsheets, WhatsApp threads and a paid CRM that no one had ever fully adopted. Like a lot of fast growing trade businesses, the tooling had grown organically as the business grew, and it was no longer fit for purpose.

The cost of that mess

About 8 to 10 hours of Cai's own time every week disappearing into admin reconciliation. Quote follow-up slipping through the gaps, costing an estimated four to six thousand pounds a month in unconverted enquiries. Engineer payroll eating a full Monday morning of office time every week and triggering repeated disputes. An SSAIB compliance audit on the horizon, with no system in place to handle the documentation load.

What we built

A purpose-built operational platform that replaced the previous CRM entirely. Quotes, jobs, customers, sites and engineer activity now sit inside a single advisory queue that surfaces what needs attention every morning. On top of that:

  • A timesheet and payroll engine with GPS and telematics verification, auto-finalising every Sunday night
  • An engineer PWA for field staff, with per-site clock-in, photos, signatures and voice-to-text job notes
  • Quote Studio, a multi-model AI pricing engine that runs across leading models and uses a separate critic model to validate every output before it reaches the client
  • An AI Compliance Manager built around SSAIB and BAFE standards, with structured engineer inputs and human sign-off baked in
  • A website with automated enquiry handling that pulls leads straight into the advisory queue

Results

  • ChatGPT subscription cancelled, which was Cai's own personal success measure for the project
  • £480 per month CRM subscription cancelled in the first month
  • Six to eight hours per week of director time returned to fee-earning work
  • Payroll Monday morning reduced from a full morning to under an hour
  • SSAIB compliance audit passed with strong positive feedback, turning a regulatory risk into a market asset
  • Operational expansion to three additional European cities now possible, because the platform handles multi-currency and multi-timezone work natively
